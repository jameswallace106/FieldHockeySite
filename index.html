Virginia Tech Club Field Hockey

Welcome to the website of VT Field Hockey! This page should lead you to find everything that you need to know about us.

Overview

VT Field Hockey is a student run organization that was founded in 2004. We practice and compete around the academic year. VTFH is a full member of the National Field Hockey League, which hosts tournaments twice a year. We have won three times (2017 Fall, 2018 Spring, 2022 Spring).
