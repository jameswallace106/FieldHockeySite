Virginia Tech Club Field Hockey

Welcome to the home page of VT Field Hockey! This website should help find everything that you need to know about us.

Overview

VT Field Hockey is a student run organization that was founded in 2004. We practice and compete around the academic year. VTFH is a full member of the National Field Hockey League, which hosts a national tournament once each semester. We have won the championship three times (2017 Fall, 2018 Spring, 2022 Spring).
